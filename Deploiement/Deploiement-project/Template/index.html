Customer satisfaction

Some subscribers complain about the poor network coverage, voice call quality, internet service etc, while others are satisfied with quality of services. The aim of this project is to conduct a survey based study to identify and highlight major attributes that effect customer's satisfaction which will eventually help telecom service providers improve their customer experience management index. Consequently, this project examines the factors that have resilient or fragile influence on customer satisfaction in order to predict it.
